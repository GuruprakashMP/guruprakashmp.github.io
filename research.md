## PhD Research *(2025 – Present)*

**Data Driven Stereodivergent Cu-NHC Catalysed Borylative Couplings for Enantioselective Skipped Dienes**

[CiQUS, University of Santiago de Compostela](https://www.usc.es/ciqus/en) | Supervisor: [Dr. Martín Fañanás-Mastral](https://fananasmastralgroup.com/)

As a **Marie Curie PhD Fellow** in the [CATALOOP Doctoral Network](https://www.tu-chemnitz.de/cataloop/), my research focuses on developing stereodivergent copper-NHC catalytic systems for borylative coupling reactions, combining:

- Experimental asymmetric catalysis and organic synthesis
- Computational chemistry (DFT) for mechanistic understanding
- Data-driven approaches using machine learning for catalyst optimization
